Food Forest Collective

Co-creating a perennial food forest that nourishes families, teaches regenerative skills, and anchors the land in abundance.

Project Intentions

The Food Forest Collective gathers designers, growers, and families to transform unused acreage into an edible ecosystem. We are layering fruit trees, shrubs, medicinal plants, pollinator meadows, and living classrooms that invite kids and elders alike to dig in.

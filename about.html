About the TEA Foundation

TEA stands for the villages we build: Thoughtful, Tranquil, Tasteful communities where transcendence, earth stewardship, and artistry meet.

Our Mission

The TEA Foundation exists to cultivate a living village—a weave of families, artists, builders, elders, and children sharing meals, dance floors, creative studios, and gardens. We steward land in Lyons, Colorado as a hub for regenerative projects, sober daytime dance gatherings, and intergenerational learning. Every initiative embraces playfulness, presence, sustainability, and community care.

TEA Pillars

T · Thoughtful & Transformative

T invites Thoughtful design, Tranquil living, Transformative experiences, and Terrestrial stewardship. We host Temple moments, Therapeutic gatherings, Trail explorations, and Third-Eye inspiration that keep the village grounded and expansive.

E · Ecstatic & Educational

E celebrates Ecstatic art, Elegant expression, Embodied learning, and Earth-centered Engineering. We curate multi-sensory Entertainment, Ecology workshops, and Entheogenic wisdom shares that nourish the mind and body.

A · Abundant & Aligned

A stands for Abundance, Adventures, Aesthetics, Alignment, and Activation. We cultivate Art residencies, Agrarian projects, Astral storytelling, and Aftercare practices that keep our communities resourced and resilient.

Village Commitments

We treat the village as a living sketch. Our agreements keep evolving, yet several commitments already guide how we gather and create together:

Care for land, children, and elders with shared responsibility Hold conflict in conversation—voice agreements verbally and clearly Design sober daytime dance spaces and family-friendly celebrations Share food, skills, music, and movement as gift economies Invite aligned collaborators through transparent application pathways
